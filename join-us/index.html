Join LoboSec

We're a student-led Capture The Flag (CTF) team at the University of New Mexico, and we welcome everyone — whether you're brand new to cybersecurity or already a seasoned hacker.

No experience? No problem. LoboSec is a place to learn, explore, and grow your skills in a supportive and collaborative environment. We’ll help you dive into topics like web exploitation, reverse engineering, cryptography, forensics, and more — one flag at a time.

If you're curious, motivated, and ready to be part of a team that learns by doing, you're in the right place.

How to Get Started
